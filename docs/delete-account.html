Home Privacy Policy

If you want your MyRAJourney account deleted, you can request deletion by email.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered email address.
  2. Use the subject line Delete My MyRAJourney Account.
  3. Include your name, registered email address, phone number, and role in the app.

Request Account Deletion

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile and login access.
  • Associated personal information stored for app use, subject to retention requirements.

What May Be Retained

  • Records required for legal, security, audit, or compliance reasons.
  • Operational logs or backup data retained for a limited period where necessary.

Deletion Timeline

Deletion requests are typically reviewed and processed within 7 to 15 business days.
