MyRAJourney is a healthcare support application designed to help users manage rheumatoid arthritis through symptom tracking, rehabilitation support, appointments, reports, medications, and educational content.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, phone number, and role.
  • Profile information such as age, gender, and address where provided by the user or administrator.
  • Health-related information including symptoms, reports, medications, CRP values, rehabilitation progress, and appointment details.
  • Technical information required for app operation, diagnostics, and security.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To provide role-based access for patients, doctors, and administrators.
  • To support symptom tracking, report review, medication management, rehabilitation, and appointment workflows.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and security.

3. Data Sharing

User information is shared only as needed to deliver the app’s healthcare features, such as allowing doctors and administrators to access patient information within the app. We do not state or represent that personal data is sold to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain user and medical workflow data only for as long as required to operate the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their account and associated personal data, subject to any legal or operational retention requirements.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization or supervision where required.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
